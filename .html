About Us

"The Sunshine Institute where Success Get Easily"


Sunshine has been guiding students to success since 2015. With a legacy of over 7 years, 50+ centers across India, 200+ expert faculty and 50k lakh+ happy students, Sunshine is well known for its comprehensive, result-oriented in secondary and Higher Secondary preparations . BYJU’S - India’s largest ed-tech company and a world leader in technology based learning solutions, now joins hands with Sunshine, to give you an unmatched learning experience with an unbeatable edge. With BYJU’S sharing our values and vision, we are excited to continue our efforts in improving our existing offering to students, by leveraging our strengths and delivering increased value and working in the direction of launching innovative products and courses. Our core objective continues to be to help you succeed and to bring you a learning experience that helps you achieve your academic goals. Sunshine Educational Services Limited (SESL) is a leading educational institution in India that provides comprehensive test preparatory services to students preparing for B.com and It entrance exams, school/board exams, KVPY, NTSE, Olympiads and other Foundation level exams. The first centre under ‘Sunshine’ was started in 2015 by Mr Pravin, offering coaching services for medical entrance examinations. Today, we are highly acknowledged for offering niche test preparatory services for IT as well a commerce sector entrance exams and foundation level exams through our distinct verticals namely, sunshine IT and Sunshine Foundations. We have a pan-India network of 50+ Sunshine Centres and a Student Count of more than 50,000.
